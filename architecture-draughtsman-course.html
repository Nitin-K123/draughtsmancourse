Architecture Draughtsman Course

The Architectural Drafting program is intended to prepare students for careers as drafters or related occupations in industry, as well as transfer to an architectural program. Drafting technicians are in short supply, so there are plenty of opportunities for qualified people. The program provides students with entry-level computer and architectural drafting skills and knowledge.

Competencies include AutoCAD-generated drawings, current architectural drafting, and construction technology practices, 2-D and 3-D drawings, and drafting techniques such as clarity, line weight expression, and accuracy, to name a few.

Course Overview:

Our Architectural Drafting Course and architectural drawing will teach you the technology and techniques required to create working architectural drawings for both residential and commercial projects. Learn how to draw conceptual design drawings and models, as well as how to prepare building permit drawings. Building science, the Ontario Building Code, and computer-aided drafting and design (CADD) are all topics to learn if you want to work in the architectural design industry.

This Architectural Drafting Class is designed for people who want to design their own living spaces as well as those who want to pursue additional Construction and Building Technologies courses and credentials.

Who Are Eligible ?

For admission, students must meet the Architecture eligibility requirements.

  • Students must have passed the 10+2 examination from a recognized board.
  • A minimum of 50% in their 10+2th examination is required.
  • Students must have completed 10+2 physics, chemistry, and mathematics courses.
  • Diploma students can also pursue this course.

Why Consider Us ?

Our Architectural Drafting and Design course prepares students to work as architectural draftspeople in the building construction industry. The program provides in-depth technical instruction in architectural drafting techniques and procedures for residential and commercial structures.

The coursework teaches students how to use the most recent construction innovations as well as current building codes and specifications. The curriculum includes topics such as beam loading calculation, heating insulation, soundproofing, sanitary systems, soil problems, and so on. Students learn about computer-aided drafting and design systems as well as basic operational applications of computer graphics. We train the students for AUTO Cad 2D &3D in architecture domain which will be an added advantage to excel in their career.



Apart from that we offer flexible timings with online and offline classes as well.

Our architecture drawing lessons are best for beginners too who want to make a career in Architecture domain.

We also Provide training for NATA entrance exam,Nata Crash course option also avilable.

Our expert faculty members and premium study material will guide you in depth.

The curriculum also includes an overview of Technical Mathematics, which is required in mechanical trade programs.

Civil Draughtsman Course

Excellent Opportunity to learn Civil Draughtsman course from Mangalmurti classes in Ambernath Draughtsman Civil is an ITI trade offered by the Directorate General of Training under the auspices of the Government of India's Ministry of Skill Development and Entrepreneurship. The drafter is another term for civil draughtsman. It is an art vocational trade, and the course is designed to provide project drawings, as well as visual guidelines and technical details. Engineers, architects, and scientists provide drafters with drawings, rough sketches, codes, and calculations.

Course Overview:

Civil Draughtsman course, also known as drafter. This course is an art vocational trade, and the syllabus is designed to provide project drawings. The ITI program is always the most effective way to learn vocational skills. Technical handbooks, tables, calculators, and computers are among the drafting tools and mathematics required to complete drawings. Candidates who complete the course will have opportunities in various government and private organizations, as well as a lucrative salary package. Many ITIs and colleges in the Mumbai offer ITI Draughtsman l courses.

Who Are Eligible ?

Students Who Have Completed Their Tenth Grade With Any Recognized Board Are Eligible.

Students Must Meet The Following Eligibility Criteria To Pursue An ITI (Industrial Training Institutes) Course :

  • Candidates must have passed the 10th-grade board exam.
  • Students who have completed their tenth grade with a recognized board.
  • Applicants must have a minimum of 50% in their 10th-grade examination.
  • Candidates have no physical disabilities.
  • Students have good vision and hearing.

Career Opportunities:

Upon the completion of the course, students can apply for the jobs of Civil Engineer, Field Engineer, Construction Manager, Site Inspector & Supervisor and more.

Why Consider Us ?

India's construction industry is booming. Many top government construction projects like airports are among many, that demonstrate a high demand for drafters. The Draughtsman is hired to turn ideas into "maps" for construction work such as buildings, machines, computer chips, and roads, among other things.
